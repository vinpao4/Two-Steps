Description

The innovative 'Two Steps' shoe box is a revolutionary product that transforms the way we transport and organise our shoes. Consisting of two perfectly sized parallelepipeds, this box is designed to hold one shoe on the left side and one on the right side.

Made of high-quality materials, the box has an elegant and sophisticated ivory finish. The two parts of the box attach via an ingenious tab system, ensuring a secure and protective closure for your shoes.

The parallelepiped dimensions have been carefully calibrated to fit most shoe sizes, allowing you to comfortably transport shoes of all sizes. In addition, the box's clever design reduces the space occupied in your luggage, allowing you to optimise organisation during your travels.

In addition to its functionality, the 'Two Steps' box stands out for its elegant and refined appearance. The ivory colour lends a touch of class, while the two-part attachment system adds an element of interest and practicality.

By choosing the 'Two Steps' box, you not only protect your shoes during transport, but also enhance their style and presence. Every detail has been taken care of to offer you the best possible experience, from the quality of the materials to the ease of use.
